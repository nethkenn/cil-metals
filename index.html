About Us

CIL Metals established last April 2017 and was formerly “Song Sun Metal Machine Shop”. Under new management of Ms.Jenny Laureta, CIL Metals moved in more spacious shop and added more machines to provide different kinds of models and variety metal work projects to satisfy our customer’s quality requirements. C.I.L Metals has now 31 units of machines such as CNC PRESS BREAK, POWER PRESS, LATHE MACHINE and SURFACE GRINDING and more.

CIL Metals has different services including metal fabrication, metal stamping, CNC press brake bending and metal equipment services. We fabricate and stamped based on the sample finished product, engineering drawings and upon on our client’s request. We also specialized welding, punching, shearing includingsub-contract manufacturing.

CIL Metals envision to be more competent and consistent in satisfying our customer’s demand. We want to broaden our business opportunities by offering high quality and guaranteed products and services to our clients.
